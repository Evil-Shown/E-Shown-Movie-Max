# CHITHRA - CINEMA v2.2.6

## 🎭 Your Account, Your Experience

We're thrilled to introduce **user accounts** — the biggest update yet. Chithra Cinema is no longer just a streaming app; it's _your_ personal cinema.

### ✨ What's New

**🔐 Sign In & Create Account**
You can now create your own account or sign in with just a few clicks. A beautiful new login window appears right when you need it — no page redirects, no hassle.

**👤 Your Profile, Everywhere**
Choose your avatar, set your display name, and carry your watchlist and progress across all your devices. Your profile icon appears in the top-right corner — click it to go to your dashboard.

**❤️ Watchlist is Now Yours**
Save movies and series to your personal watchlist. It's tied to your account, so it follows you from your phone to your desktop to the web.

**▶️ Seamless Playback**
Click "Watch" and if you're not signed in, we'll gently prompt you to log in. Once you do, the movie starts playing automatically — no need to click again.

**🎨 Login Experience You'll Love**
Dark cinematic design with smooth animations. Clear success messages when you sign in, and helpful error messages if something goes wrong.

### 🔧 Improvements

- The app is now more stable with a redesigned backend
- Faster loading times and better search performance
- Your profile name shows up everywhere instead of "Watcher"
- No more dropdown clutter — one click takes you to your dashboard

### 🐛 Fixed

- Fixed an issue where closing the login window could accidentally start playing a movie
- Various stability improvements under the hood
